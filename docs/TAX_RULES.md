# QuidSafe — Tax Rules, Rates & HMRC References

## Purpose

This document is the source of truth for all tax rates, thresholds, and HMRC references displayed in the QuidSafe app. It must be updated at the start of each tax year (6 April). All rates shown in the app MUST link to official HMRC sources.

---

## 1. Current Tax Year: 2026/27

**Tax year:** 6 April 2026 – 5 April 2027

### Income Tax Rates (England, Wales & NI)

| Band | Taxable Income | Rate |
|------|---------------|------|
| Personal Allowance | Up to £12,570 | 0% |
| Basic Rate | £12,571 – £50,270 | 20% |
| Higher Rate | £50,271 – £125,140 | 40% |
| Additional Rate | Over £125,140 | 45% |

**Note:** Personal Allowance reduces by £1 for every £2 earned over £100,000. At £125,140 it reaches zero.

**HMRC Source:** https://www.gov.uk/income-tax-rates

## 2. Making Tax Digital (MTD) for Income Tax

### Rollout Timeline

| Date | Who Must Comply | Threshold |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| **6 April 2026** | Sole traders & landlords | Gross income over £50,000 |
| **6 April 2027** | Sole traders & landlords | Gross income over £30,000 |
| **6 April 2028** | Sole traders & landlords | Gross income over £20,000 |
| **TBC** | Partnerships | Not yet confirmed |

**HMRC Source:** https://www.gov.uk/guidance/check-if-youre-eligible-for-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax

### Quarterly Submission Deadlines (2026/27)

| Quarter | Period | Deadline |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Q1 | 6 Apr – 5 Jul 2026 | 7 August 2026 |
| Q2 | 6 Jul – 5 Oct 2026 | 7 November 2026 |
| Q3 | 6 Oct – 5 Jan 2027 | 7 February 2027 |
| Q4 | 6 Jan – 5 Apr 2027 | 7 May 2027 |
| End of Period Statement | Full year | 31 January 2028 |
| Final Declaration | Full year | 31 January 2028 |

**HMRC Source:** https://www.gov.uk/guidance/using-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax

### Penalty Points System

| Situation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Late quarterly submission | 1 penalty point per missed deadline |
| Reach 4 points | £200 penalty |
| Each subsequent late submission | Additional £200 |
| **Year 1 grace (2026/27)** | No penalty points for late quarterly updates. Late annual return still incurs points. |
| Late payment (within 15 days) | No penalty (30 days grace in first year) |
| Late payment (16-30 days) | 2% of outstanding amount |
| Late payment (31+ days) | 2% + additional 2% + 4% annualised daily rate |

**HMRC Source:** https://www.gov.uk/guidance/late-submission-penalties

### What a Quarterly Update Contains

A quarterly update is NOT a tax return. It is a simple summary of:
- Total income for the quarter
- Total allowable expenses for the quarter
- Period start and end dates

You can correct errors in subsequent quarterly updates. The final declaration at year-end confirms the full position.

**HMRC Source:** https://www.gov.uk/guidance/using-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax#quarterly-updates

## 3. What Happens After You Submit

### After a Quarterly Update

1. **Immediate:** HMRC sends a digital acknowledgement with a receipt ID
2. **Within 72 hours:** Submission appears in your HMRC online account
3. **If errors found:** You can correct in the next quarterly update — no need to amend the submitted one
4. **Tax impact:** No tax payment is due after a quarterly update. Payment dates remain the same (31 Jan and 31 Jul)
5. **In QuidSafe:** Shows "Accepted" status with HMRC receipt ID

### After the Final Declaration (Year-End)

1. **Immediate:** HMRC confirms receipt
2. **Within 72 hours:** Tax calculation available in your HMRC account
3. **Tax owed:** Payment due by 31 January following the tax year
4. **Payments on Account:** If tax bill > £1,000, HMRC may require two advance payments (31 Jan and 31 Jul)
5. **If you disagree:** You can appeal within 30 days via HMRC

### If a Submission is Rejected

| Reason | What to Do |
|--------|-----------|
| Invalid data format | QuidSafe auto-validates — this shouldn't happen |
| Authentication expired | Reconnect HMRC Government Gateway in Settings |
| Duplicate submission | Already submitted for this period — no action needed |
| Server error (HMRC down) | QuidSafe will retry automatically. Check status in-app. |

**HMRC Source:** https://www.gov.uk/guidance/using-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax

## 8. Update Process

At the start of each tax year (April):
1. Check HMRC for updated rates and thresholds
2. Update `tax-years.ts` config in codebase
3. Update this document
4. Update the Tax Rules screen in the app
5. Push notification to all users: "Tax rates updated for 2027/28"
6. Blog post explaining what changed in plain English
