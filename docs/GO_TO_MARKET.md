# QuidSafe — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "Jess the Gig Worker"
- Age: 22-35
- Works: Deliveroo + occasional cleaning jobs
- Income: £18,000-£30,000/yr from multiple sources
- Tech: iPhone, uses Monzo/Starling, active on TikTok
- Pain: "I have no idea what I owe in tax and January terrifies me"
- Budget: Will pay £9.99/mo to remove that stress

### Secondary Persona: "Dave the Side Hustler"
- Age: 30-45
- Works: Full-time job + Etsy shop / tutoring / dog walking
- Income: £5,000-£15,000/yr side income
- Tech: Mixed iPhone/Android, uses high street bank
- Pain: "Do I even need to do a tax return for my side income?"
- Budget: Will pay £9.99/mo if it genuinely saves time and removes stress

## Launch Strategy (3 Phases)

### Phase 1: Seed (Month 1-3) — Target: 500 users
**Goal:** Validate product-market fit with real users.

| Channel | Action | Expected Users |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Reddit | Post genuinely helpful tax answers in r/UKPersonalFinance, r/SelfEmployedUK, link to QuidSafe | 100 |
| Facebook Groups | Join Deliveroo/Uber driver groups, answer tax questions, soft-promote | 150 |
| Friends & network | Personal outreach to anyone self-employed | 50 |
| Product Hunt | Launch day post | 100 |
| Hacker News | "Show HN: I built a tax app for UK gig workers" | 100 |

**Key metric:** Do users connect their bank? Do they come back weekly?

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 4-8) — Target: 5,000 users
**Goal:** Find repeatable acquisition channels.

| Channel | Action | Budget |
|---------|--------|--------|
| TikTok/Reels | "I asked my phone how much tax I owe" reaction videos. Show the app, show the surprise, show the relief. | £0 (organic) |
| SEO content | Blog posts: "How much tax do Deliveroo drivers pay?", "Sole trader tax calculator UK 2027", "What is Making Tax Digital?" | £0 |
| Referral program | "Give a friend 1 month Pro free, get 1 month free" — viral loop inside the app | Low |
| Partnerships | Reach out to Monzo/Starling marketplace teams for listing | £0 |
| Micro-influencers | UK finance TikTokers / YouTube (£200-500 per video) | £1,000 total |

**Key metric:** CAC < £5, conversion to Pro > 10%.

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 9-18) — Target: 30,000 users
**Goal:** Paid acquisition at profitable unit economics.

| Channel | Action | Budget |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Google Ads | "Sole trader tax calculator" keywords (high intent) | £2,000/mo |
| Facebook/Instagram Ads | Target self-employed, gig economy interests | £1,500/mo |
| Accounting partnerships | Offer bulk Pro licences to small accountancy firms | £0 |
| PR | "UK startup helps gig workers avoid tax surprises" — TechCrunch, Sifted, City AM | £0 (or PR agency £1,500/mo) |
| App Store Optimisation | Keywords: "sole trader tax", "self employed tax UK", "MTD app" | £0 |

## Pricing Psychology

### Why £9.99/mo works:
- **Below the "cup of coffee" threshold** — Cheaper than a Pret flat white
- **Below competitor pricing** — FreeAgent is £19/mo, Coconut is £9/mo
- **Annual discount at £89.99** — Creates urgency ("save 30%")
- **14-day free trial builds trust** — Let them see the value, then they pay

### Conversion Triggers (Free → Pro)
1. User tries to connect 2nd bank → "Upgrade to Pro for multi-bank"
2. MTD deadline approaches → "Submit directly with Pro"
3. After 30 days on Free → In-app message showing potential savings
4. User manually categorises 10+ transactions → "Let AI do this for you"
