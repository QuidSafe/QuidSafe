# QuidSafe — How to Use the App

## Getting Started (First 5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Sign up and start your trial
- Download QuidSafe from the App Store or Google Play, or visit quidsafe.co.uk
- Tap "Start 14-day free trial"
- Sign up with your email (magic link) or Google account
- No card required to start

### Step 2: Connect your bank
- Tap "Connect my bank" and choose your bank from the list
- You'll be redirected to your bank's app to authorise read-only access
- QuidSafe can ONLY read your transactions — it can never move money
- This is handled by TrueLayer, who are FCA authorised (FRN 901096)
- You can connect multiple banks if you use more than one for business

### Step 3: Wait for the magic
- QuidSafe syncs your recent transactions (usually 30 seconds)
- Our anonymised AI categorises each transaction as income, personal spending, or business expense
- Your dashboard updates with your tax estimate
- That's it — you're set up

## Daily / Weekly Usage

### Checking your tax position
- Open the app → Dashboard shows your tax estimate front and centre
- The plain English insight tells you exactly how much to set aside this month
- Action items tell you what needs doing (deadlines, overdue invoices, etc.)

### When you earn money
- New income appears automatically via bank sync (runs daily at 6am)
- AI categorises it — if it gets one wrong, tap the transaction and correct it
- Your tax estimate updates in real time

### When you spend on business
- Business expenses are auto-detected by AI
- To add a receipt: go to Expenses → Scan Receipt → photograph it
- The expense reduces your taxable profit (and therefore your tax bill)
- The "Tax saved" metric shows how much each expense saves you

### Sending an invoice
- Go to Invoices → Create Invoice
- Add client name, description, amount, due date
- Send directly via email from the app
- QuidSafe tracks whether it's been paid, is pending, or is overdue
- Tap "Chase" on overdue invoices to send a reminder

## Quarterly (Every 3 Months)

### Submitting your MTD quarterly update
1. QuidSafe notifies you when a quarterly update is ready
2. Go to MTD tab → Review the summary (income, expenses, net profit)
3. Check the figures look right — tap "Review transactions" to see the detail
4. Tap "Submit to HMRC"
5. QuidSafe submits digitally via the HMRC MTD API
6. You'll see a confirmation screen with your HMRC receipt ID
7. The quarter shows as "Accepted" in your history

### What if the figures look wrong?
- Before submitting: edit transactions in the Income or Expenses tab
- After submitting: don't worry — you can correct errors in the next quarterly update. HMRC expects this.

### MTD quarterly deadlines (2026/27)
| Quarter | Period | Submit by |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Q1 | 6 Apr – 5 Jul | 7 August |
| Q2 | 6 Jul – 5 Oct | 7 November |
| Q3 | 6 Oct – 5 Jan | 7 February |
| Q4 | 6 Jan – 5 Apr | 7 May |

## After You Submit

### Immediately
- QuidSafe shows a confirmation screen with your HMRC receipt ID
- The submission status changes to "Accepted"
- You receive a confirmation push notification and email

### Within 72 hours
- The submission appears in your HMRC online account (login.tax.service.gov.uk)
- You can verify it there if you want to double-check

### Important: No tax payment is due after a quarterly update
- Quarterly updates are just income/expense summaries — not tax returns
- You do NOT pay tax four times a year
- Tax payment dates remain the same: 31 January and 31 July
- The quarterly updates just keep HMRC informed throughout the year

### If something goes wrong
| Situation | What happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| Submission fails (HMRC down) | QuidSafe retries automatically. You'll see "Pending" status. |
| Authentication expired | You'll be prompted to reconnect your HMRC Government Gateway |
| Figures were wrong | Correct them in the next quarterly update — no need to amend |
| HMRC rejects the submission | Rare — QuidSafe validates data before sending. Contact support if this happens. |

## Year-End (January)

### Final Declaration
1. After Q4, QuidSafe prepares your End of Period Statement
2. Review the full year summary (total income, total expenses, tax owed)
3. Add any additional income (employment, dividends, etc.) if applicable
4. Submit your Final Declaration by 31 January
5. This replaces the traditional Self Assessment tax return
6. Pay your tax bill by 31 January

### Payments on Account
- If your tax bill is over £1,000, HMRC may require advance payments
- First payment on account: 31 January (50% of estimated next year's tax)
- Second payment on account: 31 July (another 50%)
- QuidSafe calculates and reminds you of these

## Understanding the Screens

### Dashboard
- **Tax card:** The big number is what you should have set aside for tax so far
- **Expenses deducted:** Shows how expenses have reduced your tax bill
- **Business health:** Green arrow = growing vs last year. Red = declining.
- **Action items:** Tappable cards for things that need your attention
- **Quarter timeline:** Where you are in the tax year

### Income
- **Gross income:** Total money received before expenses
- **Net profit:** What you actually made after expenses (this is what gets taxed)
- **Chart:** Monthly income vs expenses trend
- **Sources:** Breakdown of where your income comes from

### Expenses
- **Total claimed:** How much you've claimed in allowable expenses
- **Tax saved:** How much those expenses have reduced your tax bill
- **Receipt scanner:** Photograph receipts to attach to expenses
- **Tip:** Suggestions for expenses you might be missing

### Invoices
- **Outstanding:** Money owed to you
- **Create/send/chase:** Full invoicing workflow
- **Statuses:** Paid (green), Pending (gold), Overdue (red)

### Tax Rules (in Learn section)
- Current tax year rates and thresholds
- What's changing next year
- Links to official HMRC sources
- All displayed in plain English

### Settings
- **Banks:** Connected accounts and sync status
- **Security:** Encryption, biometric lock, AI anonymisation
- **Notifications:** 6 toggleable notification types
- **HMRC:** Government Gateway connection status
- **Profile:** Your UTR number, data export, account deletion
