After You Submit

Here's exactly what happens after you submit a quarterly update to HMRC.

Q3 Submitted Successfully
HMRC Receipt ID: MTD-2026-Q3-8A4F2
Submitted 18 Jan 2027 at 14:32
What happens next
Immediately

HMRC sends a digital acknowledgement. Your receipt ID is stored in QuidSafe.

Within 72 hours

Submission appears in your HMRC online account at tax.service.gov.uk

No payment due now

Quarterly updates are NOT tax payments. You still pay tax on 31 Jan and 31 Jul as before.

Errors? No problem

If you spot a mistake, correct it in the next quarterly update. No need to amend.

Penalty protection

First year grace period: For 2026/27, HMRC will NOT issue penalty points for late quarterly updates. Only late annual returns incur points. QuidSafe reminds you before every deadline.

If something goes wrong
HMRC server down
QuidSafe retries automatically
Auth expired
Reconnect Government Gateway in Settings
Submission rejected (rare)
Contact support — we'll sort it
