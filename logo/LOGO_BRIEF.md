# QuidSafe — Logo Design Brief

## Brand Overview
**QuidSafe** is a UK mobile app that helps sole traders (gig workers, cleaners, tutors, Etsy sellers) track their income and know exactly how much tax to set aside. The brand voice is friendly, trustworthy, and plain-speaking.

## Name Meaning
- **Quid** = British slang for £1 (pound sterling)
- **Pot** = Savings pot / money jar — the idea of "potting away" money for tax

## Logo Concept Direction

### Option A — The Q-Shield
A stylised letter **Q** where the tail curves into or forms a shield shape. Implies protection of your money. Minimal, geometric, works at small sizes.

### Option B — Shield & Coin
A £ coin enclosed within a shield outline. Communicates "your money is protected." Clean line art style.

### Option C — Trust Lock
A padlock shape with a £ symbol integrated. Communicates "your tax is locked away safely."

## Design Requirements
- **Must work at:** 16px favicon, 44px app icon, 200px web header
- **Must work on:** White, navy (#0F4C75), and dark backgrounds
- **Style:** Modern, minimal, Soft UI — NOT clipart, NOT 3D, NOT overly playful
- **Feeling:** Trustworthy but approachable. Like a fintech, not a children's app.
- **Avoid:** Piggy banks (too childish), literal safes (too heavy), complex illustrations, gradients that don't work in mono
